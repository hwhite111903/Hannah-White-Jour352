Is Physical Music Making a Comeback?

Vinyls and CDs see stronger growth than their digital counterpart from 2021 to 2024.

By Hannah White

Over the past couple years, physical music formats like vinyl and CDs have been having a resurgence.

While digital streaming still dominates the music industry, recent data shows that physical music sales are growing at a surprising rate. Showing that just between 2021 to 2023, vinyl revenue sales rose from $1,044.6 million to $1,350.2 million.

In recent years, vinyl and CD sales have even outperformed the growth of streaming services. With an overall revenue growth of 10.5% in 2023 compared to streamings 8.1% at that same time.

This trend suggests that more people are turning back to physical music.
